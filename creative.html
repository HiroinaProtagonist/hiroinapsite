ServiceNow Service Ticket Queue
sntQueue is a technical support incident/ticket utility that uses the ServiceNow API to display service incidents, or tickets. The application demo uses a freely-available ServiceNow developer account and can be customized to work with your own developer instance or a production installation of ServiceNow.

Graduate Thesis
Facilitating Foreign Language Learning with World of Warcraft discusses considerations of foreign language learning related to using World of Warcraft to effectively develop competency in a foreign language. The proposal relies on a strategy developed by language learning expert and former opera singer Gabriel Wyner and specific in-game tools designed for language learning in World of Warcraft.
